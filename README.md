## 🚀 Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive HR Workforce Attrition Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze employee trends, identify attrition drivers, and support data-driven HR decision-making.

The dashboard provides a centralized view of workforce metrics across **1,470 employees**, enabling stakeholders to monitor attrition patterns and take proactive retention actions.

---

## 📌 Key Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends across multiple dimensions  
- Identify high-risk employee segments  
- Enable HR teams to make **data-driven workforce decisions**  
- Replace manual reporting with an automated BI solution  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Total Employees: **1,470**
- Attrition Count: **237**
- Attrition Rate: **16.12%**
- Average Salary: **6.5K**
- Average Age: **37 years**
- Job Satisfaction: **2.6 / 5**
- Work-Life Balance: **2.7 / 5**

---

## 🔍 Dashboard Insights

### 1. Department Analysis
![Department Analysis](images/department_analysis.png)
- Highest Attrition: Sales  
- Moderate: R&D  
- Lowest: HR  

📌 Insight: Sales requires targeted retention strategies  
---

### 2. Age Group Analysis
![Age Analysis](images/age_analysis.png)

- Highest Attrition: 26–35  
- Lowest: 46+  

📌 Insight: Mid-career employees are more likely to leave  

---

### 3. Tenure Analysis
![Tenure Analysis](images/tenure_analysis.png)

- Highest Attrition: 0–2 years  
- Lowest: 10+ years  

📌 Insight: Early-stage employees are at highest risk  

---

### 4. Overtime Impact
![Overtime Analysis](images/overtime_analysis.png)

- Higher attrition in overtime employees  

📌 Insight: Work-life imbalance is a key driver 
---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Strengthen onboarding & early engagement  
- Focus retention strategy on Sales department  
- Optimize workload and reduce overtime  
- Provide career growth plans for junior employees  
- Improve job satisfaction via feedback & recognition programs  

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings
- Attrition is concentrated in specific segments  
- Early tenure employees need the most support  
- Work-life balance directly impacts retention  
- BI dashboards enable faster, real-time insights
